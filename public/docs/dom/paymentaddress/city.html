PaymentAddress.city

The city read-only property of the PaymentAddress interface returns a string containing the city or town portion of the address.

Syntax

var paymentCity = PaymentAddress.city;

Value

A DOMString indicating the city or town portion of the address described by the PaymentAddress object.
